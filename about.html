About Us

Enhance Your Events with Corpit

Corporate IT Solutions, founded in 2003, is a leading full-service technology solutions provider. The company offers cost-effective solutions tailored to clients' specific needs, ensuring the success of various events and projects throughout the Asia-Pacific region. Initially established with a strong foundation in the Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions (MICE) industry.

Corporate IT Solutions has demonstrated its expertise and commitment to excellence by expanding into China, where it established Corporate IT Solutions (Shanghai) Co. Ltd. in 2006. This expansion caters to the growing demands of consumers in Asia and marks the first step in the company's regional growth strategy. In 2019, the company further expanded its footprint by establishing a presence in Myanmar, aiming to maintain and advance its position as a premier systems provider over the next five years.
